02
Meta · 2024 · Sales enablement launch

Redesigning the sales hub for 3,000 global reps.

p. 04 of 08

The problem

Sales teams were spending 8+ hours a week searching for materials. Adoption was low despite extensive content. Reps were creating unapproved materials, creating compliance risk.

Search, not content, was the adoption blocker.

What I did

Reframed the project as a productivity and sales effectiveness play to secure executive buy-in. Assembled and led Eng, UX, Research, Design, Web Dev, and Sales through a complete platform redesign prioritizing user workflow.

8.84%less call prep time
2.75%sales lift, heavy users
71%traffic QoQ
03
Meta · 2025 · Gen AI integration

Integrating Gen AI without breaking the source of truth.

p. 06 of 08

The problem

An internal AI tool trained on outdated materials was providing incorrect information, threatening sales effectiveness and undermining the authority of the knowledge repository.

Chose a hybrid approach: AI convenience, knowledge integrity intact.

What I did

Partnered with the Gen AI team, Engineering, Product, and Sales to coordinate model retraining. When direct KB usage dropped, pivoted the AI to link every answer back to source material.

3.41%repo traffic via AI
100%knowledge integrity
Q1post-launch
04
Meta · 2024 · Video & PDF repository

Making the content people already made actually used.

p. 08 of 08

The problem

Sales teams were asking for more video despite low engagement with existing content. The real issue: discoverability, not production volume.

Acted as product owner. Advocated for new tagging when Eng wanted to reuse legacy infra.

What I did

Conducted user research, took product ownership of the repository redesign, partnered with Engineering, UX, Web Dev, and Content Managers to redesign discovery from the ground up.

691%lift in video views
57%more PDF downloads
ROIon existing content
